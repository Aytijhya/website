HELLO I'm

Aytijhya Saha

I am a final year student of the Master of Statistics (M.Stat) program at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata. My Master's dissertation is being advised by Professor Aaditya Ramdas and Professor Soumendu Sundar Mukherjee. During the summer of 2024, I was a Visiting Research Scholar in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was mentored by Professor Aaditya Ramdas. I completed my Bachelor of Statistics (B.Stat) from ISI Kolkata in 2023. I am broadly interested in statistics and machine learning.
