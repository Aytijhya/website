Aytijhya Saha

I am a final year student of the Master of Statistics (M.Stat) program at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Aaditya Ramdas and Professor Soumendu Sundar Mukherjee on my master's dissertation. During the summer of 2024, I was a Visiting Research Scholar in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was mentored by Professor Aaditya Ramdas. During the summer of 2023, I worked in the Machine Learning cohort at the Big Data Summer Institute, School of Public Health, University of Michigan. I earned my Bachelor of Statistics (B.Stat) from ISI Kolkata in 2023. My research interests span a broad spectrum within statistics and machine learning.
